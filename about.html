ABOUT ME

I’m an 18-year-old with a passion for web development and coding. I’ve always wanted to find out how different things work, and by learning to code and learning more about technology, I get a better insight and understanding of how the big world of IT and technology works behind the scenes. Right now, I’m working for Oslo Kommune Utviklings- og Kompetanseetaten as an apprentice as part of my education. Here I’m working with the Microsoft Power Platform, and using Power Apps, Power Automate and Power BI as tools in my everyday work tasks. When I’m not at work, you will mostly find me exercising, both in the way of pumping iron at the gym and cycling through the scenic routes in my area on my road bike. Otherwise, you will find me playing video games of all sorts, playing the guitar, or diving into the enormous video library of YouTube, continuously expanding my knowledge and staying up-to-date with the latest tech trends, or any other topic for that matter.
